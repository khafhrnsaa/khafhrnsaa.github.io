About Me

Halo! I'm a creative professional blending contemporary design with traditional Indonesian influences. With a passion for vibrant colors and bold layouts, I create digital experiences that tell stories and evoke emotions.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring Surabaya's street art scene, collecting vintage batik patterns, or experimenting with augmented reality projects. My work is a celebration of cultural fusion – where East meets West, and tradition dances with technology.
